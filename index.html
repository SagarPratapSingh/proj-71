Campus Life

School Library

Was established in 1954

This is my School Library. There are many books in here, and one can barrow them for a time being and then return them. Their are always two classes in the library at a period. It also have a theater, where our teachers show us educational videos.

School PlayGround

Was established in 1952

This is our School playground. Here we can play all types of games, including sports or board games. All the things for playing them are given in the playground from the School only, which we have to give back after our P.T. Period. Our School playground is also open everyday at 5pm, so students can play at evening time.

School Auditorium

Was established in 1949

This is our School auditorium. Here Dance, Solo singing, Singing and playing musical instruments are often held. Every year, near the month of November, we have celebrations here for Annuals. Also, their are Marches held for Republic Days.
